# E-commerce Customer Order Analysis & Customer Segmentation

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchase behavior and product performance using Python. The objective is to identify high-value customers, top-performing product categories, and customer spending patterns to support business decision-making.

## Business Problem

An e-commerce company wants to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify profitable product categories, and segment customers based on spending patterns.

## Dataset

The project uses customer order data containing:

- Customer Name
- Product Name
- Product Category
- Purchase Amount

## Analysis Performed

- Customer Spending Analysis
- Revenue Analysis
- Category Performance Analysis
- Customer Segmentation
- Electronics Buyer Analysis
- Top Customer Identification

## Visualizations

- Customer Spending Analysis
- Revenue by Product Category
- Customer Segmentation
- Top Customer Analysis
- Revenue Distribution

## Key Findings

- Identified high-value customers.
- Determined top-performing product categories.
- Segmented customers based on spending behavior.
- Identified customers purchasing across multiple categories.

## Business Impact

Enabled better customer targeting, improved understanding of purchasing behavior, and supported data-driven sales strategies.
